reflector magazine

visual companion to the reflector paper

Version: 0.1.0

Purpose:

Transform the reflector research paper into a highly visual, emotionally engaging, and broadly accessible magazine artifact.

The magazine is not intended to replace the paper.

The paper provides rigor.

The magazine provides understanding.

The magazine should communicate the core concepts of reflector to:

- engineers
- sponsors
- researchers
- hiring managers
- students
- AI practitioners
- curious readers

without requiring them to read the full paper.

---

Format

Total Pages: 14

Structure:

- Front Cover
- Inside Cover
- 10 Content Pages
- Inside Back Cover
- Back Cover

The magazine should feel like:

«a collectible artifact from a thoughtful future.»
