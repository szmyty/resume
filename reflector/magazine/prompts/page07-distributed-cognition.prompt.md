Title

distributed cognition

Purpose

Introduce the idea that cognition can be distributed across people, artifacts, repositories, specifications, and systems.

This page explains one of the foundational ideas behind Reflector:

Understanding does not live exclusively inside individual minds.

Understanding can be externalized into artifacts.

Repositories become memory.

Specifications become intent anchors.

Artifacts become compressed cognition.

Reflector operates within this distributed cognitive system.
